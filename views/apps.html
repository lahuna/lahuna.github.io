The Apps

Web Apps to Improve Your Internet Experience


Foto

The Foto web app gives you more visibility into exactly what photos you have stored on Google+. It allows you to search, sort, and view your Google+ albums and photos on any device using a web browser. It also gives you the ability to delete, move, merge, organize, and update your Google+ albums and photos.


Vida

What Foto does for Google+ photos and albums, Vida does for Youtube videos and playlists. Vida goes beyond standard Youtube capabilites, allowing you to search and tag playlists you have created. Vida provides a clean and simplified user interface so you can find what you are looking for with ease and enjoy it more with minimal distractions.


Blitz

The Blitz web app allows you to post text, photos, and vides to your favorite social media sites like Facebook, Blogger, Imgur, and Reddit all with the click of a button. No need to visit individual web sites or apps, just use Blitz to do it all in one place!
